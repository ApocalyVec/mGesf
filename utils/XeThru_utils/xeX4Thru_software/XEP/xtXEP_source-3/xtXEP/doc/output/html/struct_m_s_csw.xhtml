§ dCSWDataResidue

uint32_t MSCsw::dCSWDataResidue

For Data-Out the device shall report in the dCSWDataResidue the difference between the amount of data expected as stated in the dCBWDataTransferLength, and the actual amount of data processed by the device.

For Data-In the device shall report in the dCSWDataResidue the difference between the amount of data expected as stated in the dCBWDataTransferLength and the actual amount of relevant data sent by the device. The dCSWDataResidue shall not exceed the value sent in the dCBWDataTransferLength.
